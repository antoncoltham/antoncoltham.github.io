Learn About Papa's Games!
The collection of Papa's Games are a beloved franchise of online simulation and restaurant management games created by Flipline Studios. Players can run restaurants, bake desserts, and serve customers while exploring fun gameplay mechanics. Each game offers a unique theme and challenges that test your multitasking and management skills.

History

Flipline Studios - the founding company - is an American game development company founded in 2004 that is best known for its series of Papa Louie restaurant time-management games.
It was founded by Matt Neff and Tony Solary.

Gameplay

There are currently 14 games in the Papa's web series, alongside 3 Papa Louie side games.
The oldest of the franchise being Papa's Pizzeria, and the newest being Papa's Scooperia, which was released in 2018.

A Brief History
During 2002-2003, before Flipline IDS was founded, Neff and Solary worked together on a Sidescrolling/RPG game called The Mawglin. In 2004, Neff and Solary formed Flipline IDS.

Their first product was a comic called Freezing Pointe. Freezing Pointe was discontinued in 2006 when the company began their focus on creating Flash games. Flipline IDS was renamed to Flipline Studios.

Papa Louie: When Pizzas Attack! was released in the same year. On August 7, 2007, Flipline Studios unveiled their website and released their first Gameria, Papa's Pizzeria.
