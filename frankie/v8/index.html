Pollution and Recycling

This website teaches you about Pollution and Recycling!

Introduction

Pollution is when the air, water, or land get dirty or unhealthy. This can happen because of rubbish, chemicals, or smoke. Pollution happens when we don't take care of our planet. Pollution can affect humans, plants, and animals. Imagine you had a beautiful playground but people left lots of rubbish or a car drove past and left lots of smoke. This would make the playground not fun and unsafe to play in.

Water Pollution

Water pollution happens when rubbish, chemicals, or dirt get into water making it unsafe for animals, humans, and plant life.

More information: Water Pollution

Air Pollution

Air pollution happens when smoke, gases, and chemicals fill the air, making it hard to breathe and harmful for people and animals.

More Information: Air Pollution

Land Pollution

Land pollution happens when rubbish and waste are left on the ground, making the land dirty and unsafe for animals and plants.

More information: Land Pollution

Pollution is a big problem for the world, but don't worry as there are many things you can do to help!
