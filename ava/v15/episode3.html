Revenge of the Sith

Three years later, Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi and Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker rescue Chancellor Palpatine from General Grievous. Suspicions are raised concerning Chancellor Palpatine, with whom Anakin has formed a bond. When asked to spy on the chancellor, Anakin embraces the Dark Side.
