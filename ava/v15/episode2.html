Attack of the Clones

Ten years after the events of "The Phantom Menace," a separatist movement poses new threats to the galaxy that even the Jedi cannot stem. These moves, long planned by an unrevealed force, lead to the beginning of the Clone Wars -- and the beginning of the end of the Republic.
